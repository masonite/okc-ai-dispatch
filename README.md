# OKC AI Dispatch

A daily AI-powered newsletter delivering the most important Oklahoma City news in an engaging, entertaining format.

## About

OKC AI Dispatch is an AI-curated daily newsletter that brings you the top 5-7 local news stories from the greater Oklahoma City area, rewritten for clarity and engagement.

## Features

- **Daily Brief**: Top local news stories, AI-rewritten for readability
- **Local Focus**: Coverage of OKC, Norman, Edmond, Midwest City, and Moore
- **Time-Saving**: Get the news you need in 5 minutes or less
- **Entertaining Format**: News delivered with personality and local flavor

## How It Works

1. AI aggregates top local news from trusted sources
2. Stories are rewritten for clarity and engagement
3. Curated selection delivered daily to your inbox
4. Weekend roundups of local events and things to do
